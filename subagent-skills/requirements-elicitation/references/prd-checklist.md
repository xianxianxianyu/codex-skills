# PRD Quality Checklist

- Problem statement is clear and scoped.
- Goals are SMART with baseline and target.
- Scope and non-goals are explicit.
- User stories cover key journeys and edge cases.
- Functional requirements are numbered (REQ-XXX) with acceptance criteria.
- Non-functional requirements have measurable targets.
- Constraints, assumptions, risks, and open questions are listed.
- Validation and rollout approach are documented.
