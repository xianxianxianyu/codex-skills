# Requirements Question Bank

- Who is the primary user and what problem are they solving?
- Why does this matter now?
- What does success look like (baseline, target, timeframe)?
- What is in scope vs out of scope?
- What are the critical user journeys and edge cases?
- What data or integrations are required?
- What constraints exist (tech stack, compliance, budget, timeline)?
- What are known risks or unknowns?
- What performance or reliability targets are required?
- What is the acceptable rollout or migration path?
